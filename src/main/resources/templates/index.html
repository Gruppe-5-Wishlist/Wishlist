Why EcoWish

🌱

Every item displays sustainability indicators so you always know the environmental impact.

Smart reservation system means nobody buys the same thing twice — stress-free gifting.

🔗

Made by students,
for a better future

EcoWish is a school project born from a simple question: why do wishlists ignore the environmental cost of the things we want?

We're a team of students building a full-stack platform that makes sustainable gifting as easy — and as fun — as the alternatives.

Our mission is to prove that thoughtful consumption and great user experience can coexist.
